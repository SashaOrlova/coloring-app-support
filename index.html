About the App

Coloretta lets you relax by coloring detailed illustrations directly on your device. Choose artwork from different categories, use brush and fill tools, and create your own peaceful color combinations.

  • Color illustrations with brush and fill tools
  • Explore categories such as nature, animals, cities, travel, food, and lifestyle
  • Use a simple color palette for relaxing creative sessions
  • Save your progress and continue coloring later

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the app offline?
Some features may be available offline, but downloading new content or updates may require an internet connection.

How do I restore my progress?
Open the app and check your saved artworks or studio/library section. If something is missing, contact support.

How can I report a problem?
Send us an email with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
